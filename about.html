Our Story

Discover the journey of Confluence Amazons Charity Foundation and our commitment to making a difference

Creating Lasting Impact Since 2010

Confluence Amazons Charity Foundation (CACF) was established with a vision to create a world where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has access to opportunities for growth, education, and empowerment.

Our foundation focuses on four key areas: education for persons with disabilities, entrepreneurship support for women, mental health awareness, and legal aid services for the underprivileged.

Through our dedicated programs and initiatives, we have touched thousands of lives across Nigeria, creating ripples of positive change that extend far beyond our immediate beneficiaries.

Our Founder

Meet the visionary behind Confluence Amazons Charity Foundation

Hajia Rukayat Jumai Husseini, Esq.

Visionary Leader & Social Justice Advocate

Hajia Rukayat Jumai Husseini is a lawyer, philanthropist, and social entrepreneur with over 15 years of experience in advocacy and community development. Her passion for creating equitable opportunities stems from her early experiences witnessing the challenges faced by marginalized communities.

As a legal practitioner, she has championed numerous causes, particularly focusing on the rights of persons with disabilities. Her groundbreaking advocacy led to the waiver of Post-UTME examinations for visually impaired candidates in Nigerian universities, opening doors to higher education for countless individuals.

Under her leadership, CACF has grown from a small community initiative to a nationally recognized foundation with impactful programs in education, entrepreneurship, mental health, and legal aid.

"True change happens when we move beyond sympathy to action. Every person we empower creates a ripple effect that transforms families, communities, and ultimately, our society."
